🌙 Eid Ul Adha

Eid Ul Adha is one of the holiest Islamic festivals, commemorating Prophet Ibrahim's unwavering faith and willingness to sacrifice his son in obedience to Allah. It marks the completion of Hajj pilgrimage and is a time of prayer, gratitude, charity, and joyful gatherings with loved ones.
