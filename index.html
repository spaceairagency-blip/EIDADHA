What is Eid?

Eid is an Arabic term meaning "festivity" or "festival". It represents two major holy days observed by millions globally within the Islamic calendar system.

Eid Ul Adha

This countdown is dedicated to Eid Ul Adha (the Festival of Sacrifice). It commemorates the profound devotion of Prophet Ibrahim and coincides with the completion of the holy Hajj pilgrimage.

Tradition & Culture

Families celebrate by wearing fine clothes, sharing bountiful meals, distributing charity portions to the less fortunate, and exchanging well-wishes saying "Eid Mubarak!"
